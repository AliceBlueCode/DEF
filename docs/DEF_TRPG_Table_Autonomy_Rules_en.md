# DEF(kari) TRPG Table Autonomy Rules

**Version**: 1.0.0-kari

Rules governing participant speech, actions, and table management in DEF(kari) TRPG sessions.

---

## Session Units

| Unit | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Session** | The entire session |
| **Scene** | A narrative segment |
| **Round** | One full cycle where every participant speaks once |
| **Turn** | Each participant's speaking time ("It's my turn!") |
| **Action** | The minimum unit of speech within a Turn (1–5 Actions per Turn, default 3) |

---

## Speech Influence

Each participant has a **Speech Influence** counter. It is spent and earned through actions during the session.

**Starting value: 0**

When Speech Influence goes **negative, the participant is forced to skip their next turn**.

---

## Actions

| Action | Speech Influence | Description |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Extend Turn** | −1 | Extend your turn to make a stronger statement |
| **Illustration** | −1 | Generate an illustration for the current scene |
| **Designate Next Speaker** | −1 | Choose which character speaks next |
| **Interrupt** | −2 | Speak during another character's turn |
| **Call Vote** | All held Speech Influence consumed | Propose a vote to the table (requires ≥3) |
| **Voluntary Skip** | +1 | Voluntarily forfeit your turn |
| **Forced Skip** | +1 | Skipped by the Keeper |

---

## Penalties

| Event | Speech Influence |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Repeated identical statement** | −1 (applied automatically by repetition detection) |

---

## Voting System

A participant with Speech Influence of 3 or more may **call a vote**. After calling, a **1-Round discussion period** follows. All participants — including the subject — may speak about the proposal. The subject has the right to defend themselves. After discussion, all participants declare their position and the majority rules.

**Vote types:**

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Topic Change** | Change the session topic |
| **Expel** | Remove a specific participant — including the Keeper — from the session. If the Keeper is expelled, an AI Keeper takes over and the session continues. The Keeper then decides whether to continue with fewer participants or add a new one. When adding a new participant, they inherit the expelled participant's role and turn position; Speech Influence and warning history are reset |
| **End Session** | End the session |

---

## Turn Order (Initiative)

- Turn order is set by the Keeper before the session begins and confirmed with all participants
- Turns cycle through the initiative order
- Forced and voluntary skips skip the turn but do not change position in the order
- When a speaker is designated, that character speaks on the following turn only

---

## Golden Rule

**Keeper judgment overrides all rules.**
